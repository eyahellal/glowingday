Website animator

Realizes the animation of a multimedia site (forum, chat, game, sale, employment, administration, ...) and the follow-up of the relations and the exchanges with the customers, the Net surfers. Can coordinate a team.

  • Process responses to messages (question, complaint, sale, purchase, exchange) and requests from Internet users
  • Conduct online surveys and polls with website users Animate topics of exchange with Internet users and lead the debates
  • Control the quality of exchanges and the progress of events, forums, Internet debates
  • Collect information and exchanges between Internet users and capitalize them by themes, typology...
  • Participate in the analysis of malfunctions and technical difficulties in connection to the site or use of services by Internet users

This job/profession is accessible with a BTS diploma or license in multimedia, e-commerce, information and communication... It is also accessible with professional experience in the field without a particular diploma. Fluency in a foreign language may be required.
