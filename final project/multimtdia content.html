Multimedia designing

What multimedia designer do?

Researches and defines the creative concepts of multimedia projects (communication, video games, etc.), supervises the realization of the selected projects (model, rough, storyboard) in coherence with the commercial strategy. Can coordinate a team.


What are required skills?

  • Editorial standards
  • Semiotic
  • Linguistic
  • Rules for developing a Graphic Charter
  • Management techniques
  • Accounting and administrative management

how can I obtain job?

This job/profession is accessible from a BTS or Bachelor's degree in the sectors of communication, multimedia, marketing, commerce and advertising, graphic arts, etc. Fluency in a foreign language is required. Mastery of computer creation tools (desktop publishing -PAO-, graphic palette, computer-generated image) is required.
