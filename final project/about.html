About us

Who are we?

Tunisian Repository of Trades and Skills, it is a mapping of all existing trades on the labor market, grouped by field of activity and by professional field. Each appellation corresponds to a set of knowledge to be possessed and know-how to be implemented for the exercise of the profession. It will serve as a reference tool for matching job offers nd job seekers, by establishing a common language based on skills between the two.

We aim to:


To job seekers and workers independents to get to know each other better and to identify and enhance their skills


Unify the language of the different actors of the Tunisian job market


Offer recruiters and job seekers a tool that will allow them better visibility of trades and jobs on the Tunisian market.
