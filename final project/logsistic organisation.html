What Logistics Analyst do?

Defines and implements organizational plans for all or part of a supply chain of physical flows of goods, from the purchase of raw materials to the distribution of finished products. Designs steering and management tools (procedures for tracking orders, circuits and information system, etc.) to trace the routing of products between the various actors in the supply chain (producers, suppliers, carriers, distributors , ...) with the aim of rationalization and optimization (quality, profitability, deadlines, safety). Can be specialized in a phase: supplies (upstream logistics), distribution (downstream logistics), ... or organize operations in a field (humanitarian, ...). May lead a logistics team or department.
