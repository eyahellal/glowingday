Animal care

What veterinarian do?

Carries out animal comfort care (preparation, distribution of food, cleaning of the environment, etc.) according to the needs and characteristics of the animal. Can carry out actions to assist the veterinary practitioner during veterinary care. Can carry out the administrative follow-up of the structure.


What are required skills?

  • animal biology
  • Basic elements in zoology
  • Basic elements in animal pathologies
  • Rules of hygiene and asepsis
  • Animal approach and handling techniques
